Our Solutions: The Future of Team Collaboration

Tanka is an AI messenger with long-term memory, designed to enhance team collaboration by providing context-aware replies, insights, and proactive suggestions. Experience the next generation of workplace communication.

Core Offerings

AI Messenger with Long-Term Memory

Tanka remembers past conversations and context to provide smarter, more relevant responses. Unlike traditional messaging apps, Tanka builds a knowledge base from your team's communications, ensuring no valuable information is lost over time.

Proactive & Actionable Insights

Tanka analyzes conversations to summarize to-dos, send reminders, and offer suggestions to keep teams agile and innovative. Our AI identifies patterns and opportunities that humans might miss, turning conversations into actionable intelligence.

Seamless Team Collaboration

Tanka acts as a central knowledge hub, preventing loss of institutional knowledge and ensuring all team members are aligned. New team members can quickly get up to speed, and distributed teams stay connected with shared context and understanding.

Use Cases

Engineering Teams

Track bugs, discuss features, and remember project decisions with ease. Tanka helps engineering teams maintain technical context across sprints and projects, reducing the need for repetitive explanations and documentation.

Sales & Marketing

Manage client conversations, recall deal details, and coordinate campaigns seamlessly. Tanka ensures no customer preference or campaign insight is forgotten, helping teams deliver personalized experiences at scale.

Executive Leadership

Access high-level summaries, track team progress, and make informed decisions based on comprehensive data. Tanka provides executives with the context they need without requiring them to sift through endless messages.
