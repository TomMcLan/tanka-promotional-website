The Genesis of Tanka: From a Simple Problem to a Revolution in Team Collaboration

Tanka was born from a fundamental observation: AI tools lacked long-term memory, limiting their ability to truly understand context and provide meaningful assistance over time.

Inspired by neuroscience research, we developed Tanka as the first AI messenger with persistent memory, enabling it to learn continuously from conversations and integrated apps.

Our mission is to enhance team collaboration by providing context-aware replies, proactive suggestions, and actionable insights, effectively creating an AI co-founder that remembers all business context and prevents the loss of institutional knowledge.
